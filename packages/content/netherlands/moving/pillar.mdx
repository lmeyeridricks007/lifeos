## Start with a calm plan

Use the moving hub to map your timeline. Prioritize identity docs, address setup, BSN registration, and insurance checkpoints.

## Before you move

Prepare a document pack early. Confirm validity periods and keep certified copies where needed.

## First 30 days

Focus on registration milestones and practical setup. Keep a simple tracker for appointments and document follow-ups.

## First 90 days

Transition from setup into stable routines. Review what changed and close any open admin loops.
