# Outreach targets: Institutions & official-adjacent pages

Companion to [Backlinks & promotion playbook](../backlinks-and-promotion.md) §2.1.

**Disclaimer:** Links and contacts are **starting points** for research. Verify URLs, policies, and the right recipient before outreach. **Do not** mass-mail generic templates. Inclusion here is **not** an endorsement or guarantee that any site will link to you.

**Concrete prospect list (universities, cities, embassies, etc.):** [07-concrete-outreach-prospects.md](./07-concrete-outreach-prospects.md) §A–C, **§J** (more HBO), **§K** (more cities & foreign missions).

## Outreach templates

Replace placeholders in **ALL CAPS**; delete bracketed optional lines if unused. Keep emails under **~150 words** where possible.

**Placeholders**

| Token | Example |
|-------|---------|
| `CONTACT_NAME` | “Dear International Office team,” or a named person if known |
| `ORG_NAME` | University of Amsterdam |
| `THEIR_PAGE_URL` | Exact URL you’re commenting on |
| `YOUR_PAGE_URL` | One canonical page on your site |
| `YOUR_SITE_NAME` | Your brand |
| `SPECIFIC_GAP` | One concrete gap (e.g. “order of BSN vs health insurance for non-EU”) |
| `YOUR_CREDENTIAL` | One line (e.g. “We maintain editorial guides for people moving to the Netherlands; we link to IND and official sources.”) |

### T1 — University international office (first email)

**Subject:** Suggestion for [ORG_NAME] international students page — supplementary resource

```
CONTACT_NAME,

I’ve been reading YOUR_PAGE_URL for students preparing to move to the Netherlands. The official links to IND and the gemeente are clear; I noticed there’s little on SPECIFIC_GAP in one place.

We published a plain-language guide that walks through that sequence and links only to official sources: YOUR_PAGE_URL

If it’s useful for your students, would you consider adding it under “external resources” or similar? We’re happy to adjust wording to match your editorial standards.

YOUR_CREDENTIAL

Thank you for your time,
[Your name]
[Your role]
[Email]
```

### T2 — Follow-up (7–10 days later, one time only)

**Subject:** Re: Suggestion for [ORG_NAME] international students page

```
CONTACT_NAME,

Following up once on my note from [date] about YOUR_PAGE_URL and YOUR_PAGE_URL.

No problem if this isn’t a fit—if there’s a different contact for web content or student communications, I’d be grateful for a pointer.

Best,
[Your name]
```

### T3 — ESN / student association (“useful links”)

**Subject:** Resource for newcomers — [YOUR_SITE_NAME]

```
Hi [Section name] board,

We run YOUR_SITE_NAME, a free guide for people settling in the Netherlands (registration, BSN, insurance basics). We’re not a commercial relocation service.

Would you be open to listing YOUR_PAGE_URL alongside your other newcomer links, if you maintain a “useful links” page? Happy to provide a one-line blurb in your tone.

Thanks,
[Your name]
[Email]
```

### T4 — IN Amsterdam / I amsterdam / city programme (formal tone)

**Subject:** Partner content idea — post-arrival checklist (non-duplicative)

```
CONTACT_NAME,

We appreciate YOUR_PAGE_URL as the authoritative entry point for formalities in the region.

We’ve published a short, non-official checklist focused only on SPECIFIC_GAP for people who have already booked or completed registration: YOUR_PAGE_URL. It does not duplicate IN Amsterdam procedures; it links to government sources.

If you ever curate vetted third-party resources for newcomers, we’d welcome your review. We can also provide a CC-licensed version for your editors to adapt.

YOUR_CREDENTIAL

Kind regards,
[Your name]
[Email]
```

### T5 — Gemeente / government (only if they run campaigns or editorial hubs)

**Subject:** Content offer — [topic] for [city] international residents

```
CONTACT_NAME,

I’m reaching out in case [ORG_NAME] is planning content for international residents (e.g. campaigns around registration or living in [city]).

We have YOUR_PAGE_URL — fact-checked, linking to overheid.nl / IND. We’re not soliciting a commercial partnership; we can provide copy for your team to own and edit, or a short quote for a news item.

If this isn’t the right channel, could you forward me to web communications?

Best,
[Your name]
[Email]
```

### T6 — Study in Holland / Nuffic-style portal (editorial angle)

**Subject:** Pitch: [topic] for international students — YOUR_SITE_NAME

```
CONTACT_NAME,

We’d like to pitch a short article or resource summary on SPECIFIC_GAP for students already accepted to Dutch institutions, complementary to studyinholland.nl’s official procedures.

Proposed angle: [one sentence].

Our reference guide: YOUR_PAGE_URL (we cite Nuffic/IND where relevant).

If you publish guest or partner content, please point us to your guidelines.

Best,
[Your name]
[Email]
```

### T7 — Embassy in NL (US/UK/etc. citizen resources)

**Subject:** Suggested addition — practical checklist for citizens living in the Netherlands

```
CONTACT_NAME,

On YOUR_PAGE_URL, citizens often still ask about municipality registration, BSN, and mandatory insurance order alongside consular topics.

We maintain a neutral, non-legal checklist (YOUR_PAGE_URL) that aligns with terminology used on IND.nl. If your team maintains a “living in the Netherlands” resource list, would you consider a one-line addition? We update it when major rules change and can flag changes to your web team.

No commercial service attached.

Respectfully,
[Your name]
[Email]
```

## Quick copy checklist before you send

1. Open their page—quote **one specific** gap or outdated line.  
2. Link **one** URL on your site that fixes that gap.  
3. No attachments; no “SEO” or “link exchange” language.  
4. One follow-up max.
