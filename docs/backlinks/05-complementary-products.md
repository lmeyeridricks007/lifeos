# Outreach targets: Complementary products (housing, finance, insurance)

Companion to [Backlinks & promotion playbook](../backlinks-and-promotion.md) §2.5.

**Disclaimer:** Many placements are **paid**, **affiliate**, or **partnership**—disclose per **EU consumer law** and your own **editorial policy**. Below lists **types** and **well-known examples** with public URLs; **partnerships team** contacts change—use each site’s **Partner** / **Press** / **Contact** page.

**Concrete prospect list (housing platforms, coworking, telco / insurers):** [07-concrete-outreach-prospects.md](./07-concrete-outreach-prospects.md) §H–I, **§M** (telco, health insurer).

## F. Comparison — partnership vs editorial

| Type | Likely cost | Link type | Your obligation |
|------|-------------|------------|-----------------|
| **Pure editorial** | Time only | Natural | Accuracy, no payment |
| **Affiliate** | Revenue share | Tagged / disclosed | Disclose in content |
| **Sponsored** | Fee | negotiated | Contract + disclosure |
| **Co-marketing** | Barter | Often reciprocal | Brand guidelines |

## Outreach templates

Many of these contacts go through **press@**, **partnerships@**, or web forms. Keep **compliance** in mind: frame everything as **customer education**, not financial or legal advice.

**Placeholders:** `BRAND_NAME`, `THEIR_PAGE_URL`, `YOUR_PAGE_URL`, `YOUR_SITE_NAME`, `TOPIC`, `SPECIFIC_CUSTOMER_FRICTION`.

### T1 — Housing platform / blog editor (Pararius-style)

**Subject:** Content idea — TOPIC for international renters

```
Hi [Content / marketing team],

Your readers on THEIR_PAGE_URL often hit SPECIFIC_CUSTOMER_FRICTION. We published YOUR_PAGE_URL as a non-competitive explainer (we don’t list properties).

If it reduces confused support tickets, would you consider a “Further reading” link or a short quote from us in a future blog post?

Happy to adapt the blurb to your tone.

Best,
[Your name]
[YOUR_SITE_NAME]
[Email]
```

### T2 — Bank / insurer (PR or content — formal, short)

**Subject:** Educational resource — BSN / registration order for new residents

```
Dear [Communications team],

BRAND_NAME’s page THEIR_PAGE_URL explains [product]. New arrivals often ask how that fits with gemeente registration and BSN.

We maintain YOUR_PAGE_URL — purely educational, cites your product pages where relevant. Would your team review it for accuracy? If it passes your standards, we’d be grateful if you’d consider listing it under “useful links” for internationals.

We are not a regulated financial or insurance provider; we include disclaimers.

Kind regards,
[Your name]
[Email]
```

### T3 — Comparator / marketplace (partnerships path)

**Subject:** Partnership / editorial enquiry — YOUR_SITE_NAME

```
Hi [Partnerships],

We run YOUR_SITE_NAME (guides for moving to the Netherlands). We’re interested in understanding whether BRAND_NAME works with editorial partners or comparison widgets for [topic].

We’re open to:
• Sponsored placement with clear disclosure, or
• Organic citation if your editorial team finds YOUR_PAGE_URL useful

Who is the right contact?

Thanks,
[Your name]
[Email]
```

### T4 — Telecom / utility (customer journey)

**Subject:** Resource for new tenants — energy / mobile setup order

```
Hi [Content team],

We reference THEIR_PAGE_URL when explaining when new residents typically arrange TOPIC.

Our guide YOUR_PAGE_URL walks through the sequence after lease signing. If it aligns with your customer education goals, would you consider linking it from THEIR_PAGE_URL or your blog?

[Your name]
[YOUR_SITE_NAME]
[Email]
```

### T5 — Coworking / flex office (event-first)

**Subject:** Free lunch talk — TOPIC for members

```
Hi [Community manager],

I’d like to offer a 20-minute brown-bag for members on TOPIC (first-month admin in NL for remote workers and founders). No fee.

If it works for your calendar, we can add YOUR_PAGE_URL to the event blurb as optional reading. I’ll avoid promoting anything beyond that unless members ask.

Best,
[Your name]
[Email]
[Phone]
```

### T6 — Follow-up (B2B, 10–14 days)

**Subject:** Re: [original subject]

```
Hi [Name],

Following up once on my note from [date]. If partnerships or content sits with another team, a contact would help.

Thanks either way,
[Your name]
```
